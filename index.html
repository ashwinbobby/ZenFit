Physical fitness is important because it can help you live longer and healthier
Here are some of the benifits:

Better mood: Physical activity can help you feel more energized, relaxed, and have a better mood,
and a better mood leads to a better day!

Stronger bones and muscles: Regular physical activity can help you build stronger bones, muscles, and joints,
reducing the risk of injuries and fractures, even in old age.

Lower risk of disease: Taking care of your health can help lower your risk
of developing heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, and some cancers.

Better brain function: Exercise can help improve your brain function.
The academic and career-related goals you aim to achieve will come closer and instil a feeling
of success.

Better sleep: Physical exercise during the daytime can help you sleep better. A good night's
rest is essential in ways you never would have even imagined.

Faster recovery: Regular exercise can help you recover faster from injuries or surgery quickly.

Longer life: Daily workouts and fitness routines can help you live longer and healthier.
